## THE AZIMUTHAL PROFILE FUNCTIONS
### Generalized Elipsed
The radial coordinate of the generalized ellipse is defined by:
$$
r(x,y) = \pqty{ \abs{x - x_{c}}^{C_{0} + 2} + \abs{ \frac{{y-y_{c} }}{q} }^{C_{0} + 2} }^\frac{1}{ C_{0} + 2} 
$$
- While $C_{0}=0$,  the isophotes are pure ellipses.
- While $C_{0}<0$, the shape becomes more diamond-like
- While $C_{0}>0$, the shape becomes more boxy
- 4 free parameters: $(x_{0},y_{0},q,\theta_{\mathrm{PA}})$.
- The central coordinates and position angle are inherited from the radial profile.
